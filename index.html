DARK TACTICS UNIT

Introducing a unique musical duo creating mesmerizing soundscapes at the intersection of dark music and cyberpunk. Their work immerses listeners in dark but mesmerizing worlds inspired by the atmosphere of futuristic megacities and hacking aesthetics. Combining elements of supertrap, they experiment with heavy basslines and distorted melodies, creating unique tracks that make you move and ponder at the same time. Their music is not just sounds, but an entire experience that reflects the struggle between light and darkness, man and technology. Each track tells a story about modern society, its fears and hopes, the depths of cyberspace and how technology shapes our reality. Listening to them will take you on a journey through unfamiliar but familiar places, where each beat awakens dark emotions and allows you to look at the world from a new perspective.
